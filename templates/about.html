About Us


Tama-Sopon Demo Product Co., Ltd. was founded in March 1995. We specialize in importing and distributing high-quality culinary ingredients and gourmet products, primarily sourced from France, Italy, and other parts of Europe, as well as selected regions across Asia and South America.

Our customer base spans across various sectors, including five-star hotels, Michelin-rated restaurants, artisanal bakeries, high-end grocery chains, and food manufacturing companies throughout Thailand and Southeast Asia. With over 25 years of industry experience, we are committed to building long-term partnerships with both suppliers and clients, driven by a passion for quality, innovation, and service excellence.
